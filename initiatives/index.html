Our Method

From mission need to measurable impact.

1

Identify mission need

Start from a real mission or market need defined with the partners who own it.

2

Align partners

Bring government, industry, academia, and research partners around a shared objective.

3

4

Stand up the pathway, move the first opportunities through, then expand what works.
