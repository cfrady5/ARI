## Image guidelines

- **Orientation:** portrait or square. Cards crop with `object-fit: cover`
  and center the subject automatically.
- **Recommended size:** ~500 px on the short edge or larger.

## LinkedIn links

Each card links to the person's LinkedIn profile. To change one, edit the
matching `href` in `about/index.html` (search for the `aria-label`, e.g.
`aria-label="Andrew Kossack on LinkedIn"`).
