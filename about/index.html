About ARI

The operating partner for mission-driven innovation.

ARI helps government, industry, academia, and research partners move complex ideas into executable programs, partnerships, and outcomes.

Because the Warfighter Can't Wait.

Catalyzing opportunities that enhance national security and drive economic prosperity.

ARI exists to create and facilitate opportunities for partners and stakeholders working at the intersection of national security, technology, research, and economic growth.

Enhance National Security

Move critical technologies into the hands of those defending the nation with speed and discipline.

Catalyze Opportunities

Create trusted environments where government, industry, academia, and research partners align around shared mission needs.

Drive Economic Prosperity

Convert innovation into high-value jobs, regional capacity, and durable economic growth.
